Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on a user’s device when visiting a website. Cookies and similar technologies may be used to ensure the proper operation of the Website, maintain technical settings, and support certain embedded functions.

2. What Cookies the Website May Use

At the time of preparing this Policy, the MHV Website is designed as a B2B presentation website with a contact form and an embedded map. For that configuration, the Website may use only strictly necessary cookies and similar technologies required for the basic operation of the Website and the display of the embedded mapping service.

If analytical, marketing, advertising, or other non-essential technologies are added to the Website in the future, MHV will update this Cookie Policy and, where required, implement an appropriate consent mechanism before such technologies are activated.

3. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Strictly necessary cookies may be used to support core Website functions, network security, traffic routing, technical stability, and the proper submission of the contact form. Such cookies generally do not require separate consent to the extent permitted by applicable law.

4. Embedded Third-Party Services

If the Website uses an embedded map or other third-party content, the relevant provider may place cookies or process technical data such as IP address, browser information, and interaction data relating to that embedded element.

The actual scope of processing depends on the specific service and its configuration. Users should also review the privacy documentation of the relevant third-party provider.

5. Analytics and Marketing Cookies

As of the last updated date of this Policy, MHV does not intend to use analytics or marketing cookies on the Website unless such tools are separately implemented and properly reflected in an updated version of this Policy.

If such technologies are introduced, MHV will update the description of cookie categories, processing purposes, storage periods, and, where required, user consent settings before those technologies are used.

6. Managing Cookies

Users may manage cookies through their browser settings, including blocking or deleting previously stored cookies. Restricting cookies may affect the availability or proper functioning of certain Website features.

If a cookie preference mechanism is introduced on the Website in the future, users will also be able to manage their choices through the relevant settings panel.

7. Cookie Retention

The retention period of cookies depends on their purpose and the settings of the relevant tool. Session cookies are typically deleted when the browser is closed, while persistent cookies may remain until their expiration date or until deleted by the user.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

MHV may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The current version will be published on the Website together with the updated last revision date.
