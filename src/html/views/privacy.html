Depending on the context, MHV may process the following categories of personal data: name, surname, job title, company name, email address, telephone number, country, address, inquiry content, communication data, commercial data, shipping and payment-related data, and technical data relating to Website use.

4. Sources of Personal Data

We may obtain personal data directly from you, from your company, from correspondence, from publicly available business sources, and automatically when you use the Website.

MHV processes personal data to respond to inquiries, prepare commercial proposals, conduct negotiations, conclude and perform contracts, organize logistics and deliveries, comply with legal obligations, ensure Website security, and protect the company’s legitimate interests.
The legal bases for processing may include performance of a contract, steps taken at your request prior to entering into a contract, compliance with a legal obligation, and MHV’s legitimate interests, such as conducting B2B communications, preventing fraud, protecting the company’s rights, and ensuring information security.

MHV may share personal data with IT and hosting providers, email and communications providers, accounting, tax, and legal advisers, carriers and logistics partners, and public authorities where required by law.

If personal data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, MHV will ensure that such transfer is subject to an appropriate lawful basis and safeguards under applicable data protection law.

MHV retains personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, including compliance with legal, tax, accounting, and contractual obligations, as well as dispute resolution.
Inquiry and correspondence data is retained for a reasonable period to handle the request and any related business follow-up. Contractual and accounting records are retained for the period required by law or justified by the nature of the business relationship.
